S E R V I C E S

Bike Repairs

Our friendly, experienced bycicle mechanics can perform simple repairs on punctures, gears and brakes, carry out standard servicing or even a full re-build, all at very competitive prices.

Take advantage of our fully insured repair service. From high end Mountain bikes, Hybrid bikes and road bikes, we fix any bike any size.

We offer a free 5 points safety/health check with any repair.

Bike Hire

With a stunning natural environment and amazing landscapes, Bike King Borders Hire is the best way to experience the true character of Scotland.

We can assist you with high-performance road, hybrid or mountain bikes hire in the Borders. We can also assist you with a guided ride in one of our available trails

- a ride with a local cyclist that knows the best routes, photo-ops, and post ride whiskey or coffee with Scottish sweets to ensure your Scottish cycling experience is sensational.

Our guarantee

Here at Bike King Borders we take pride in the bikes we design, build and sell and are happy that all of our bikes are of the highest quality.

That's why we offer a Limited Lifetime Warranty on all of our components of all Bike King Borders exclusive brand's for the original owner.

This means that whether you purchase a V-3, R-2, R-4,C-3 bike, we will replace any component free of charge if there is a manufacturing defect for the entire time you own the bike.

Free servicing included when purchasing any of our bikes for one year and one 5 points health/safety check.
